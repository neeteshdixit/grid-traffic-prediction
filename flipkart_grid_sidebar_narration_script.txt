"Hello judges and technical reviewers. My name is Ayushi Vyas, and today I am excited to present our Flipkart GRID Round 2 submission: TrafficAI, an Enterprise AI Traffic Demand and Parking Intelligence System. 

I have already successfully deployed this entire system in production. The Next.js frontend is live on Vercel at grid-traffic-prediction.vercel.app, the FastAPI backend is hosted on Render, and our database is hosted on MongoDB Atlas in the cloud.

Urban congestion is a massive economic drain. Existing municipal solutions are static and reactive.
