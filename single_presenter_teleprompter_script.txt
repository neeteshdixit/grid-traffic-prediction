🗣️ SPEECH SCRIPT (क्या बोलना है):
"Let's move to the Congestion Impact tab. 

To quantify the direct delay caused by illegal parking, we developed a Passenger Car Unit—or PCU—blockage engine. We normalize different vehicle classes using standard traffic engineering weights: Two-wheelers count as 0.5 PCUs, Cars as 1.0, and Trucks or Buses as 3.0 PCUs.

By taking the sum of these weights and dividing them by the lane width factor, our engine calculates the percentage of lane capacity lost. This is adjusted by a peak multiplier during rush hours to output a dynamic Congestion Score from 0 to 100. This enables traffic authorities to prioritize zones based on actual road blockage rather than simple violation counts."
